This is eduNFT.

A place where students can create their own NFTs and others can buy them.

As NFTs continue to explode in popularity, there's not much of a connection between NFTs and the education space. Thus, our idea was to create a platform to help introduce students into using cryptocurrency and NFTs.

Therefore, eduNFT was born. An online, open marketplace allowing for students ONLY to upload and create their own NFTs for sale and auction. Meanwhile, any outsider can freely browse and purchase these NFTs!
